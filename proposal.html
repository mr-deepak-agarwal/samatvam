The Work

Six milestones, each with a clear outcome.

Every milestone below ships something visible — not just backend changes you have to take our word for.

I.
Week 1–2 · Foundation

Core Rebuild & Booking Path

  • Rebuild all 6 pages — Home, About Us, Offerings, Wisdom Blog, Event Gallery, Contact — on a modern, fast platform
  • Brand kit migrated exactly: same colours, logo, fonts, and tone of voice
  • Click-to-call and WhatsApp button added to every page, mobile-sticky
  • "Book a Consultation" placed in the navigation, homepage, and footer
  • Fixed the broken Offerings / Services link inconsistency
  • Removed leftover Squarespace clutter (unused cart page, outdated footer year)
  • Built mobile-first, targeting sub-3-second load time and a 90+ mobile speed score
Goal on completion A visitor on any page, on any device, can call or message Samatvam within five seconds — without hunting for how, and without waiting for a slow page to load.
II.
Week 2–3 · Discoverability

SEO Foundation

  • Unique, written title and meta description for every page (currently blank)
  • Keyword research conducted by us — identifying the exact searches patients are typing, condition by condition
  • Page content and copy refined in close discussion with Dr Moghe, so nothing is published without her sign-off
  • Heading structure (H1/H2) corrected across the site
  • Descriptive alt text added to every image, including the Event Gallery
  • Canonical tags fixed to prevent duplicate-page confusion
  • Internal links connecting Offerings, Testimonials, and Contact
  • Initial backlink strategy mapped out — relevant Ayurveda/wellness directories and local citation sources identified for future outreach
Goal on completion Every page earns a real, written snippet on Google, built around the searches patients actually use — instead of the current blank listing that gets skipped.
III.
Week 3 · Local Search

Structured Data & Local Search Setup

  • Physician schema added — Dr Moghe's BAMS qualification, 15 years' experience, Marma massage specialty
  • LocalBusiness schema — address, hours, phone, mapped for Google
  • Google Business Profile claimed, cleaned up, and fully completed
  • Name, address, and phone number made consistent everywhere online
Goal on completion Samatvam becomes eligible to appear on Google Maps and the local pack — not just in plain organic search.
IV.
Week 3–4 · Trust

Trust & Conversion Layer

  • Testimonials (anaemia, asthma, IBS) placed near every consultation CTA, not just the homepage footer
  • Same testimonials surfaced on the relevant Offerings pages where they're most persuasive
  • Founder credentials (SVYASA certification, published Ayurvedic recipes) given proper visibility on the About page
Goal on completion Proof sits exactly where doubt would otherwise stop someone from booking.
V.
Week 4 · Measurement

Analytics & Tracking

  • GA4 installed and configured across the site
  • Call and WhatsApp click tracking set up
  • Contact form submissions tracked as a measurable goal
Goal on completion You can see, in numbers, exactly how many people called, messaged, or enquired each month — not a guess.
VI.
Launch · Quality, Handover & Ongoing SEO Begins

Testing, Launch & Handover

  • Cross-device and cross-browser testing before go-live
  • Site speed and Core Web Vitals optimisation
  • Domain connected and site submitted to Google for indexing
  • Walkthrough call so the practice can update content independently going forward
Goal on completion A fast, fully live site that the practice understands and can rely on — backed by 6 months of support that follows.
