The rebuild, line by line

What changes, exactly.

01

No phone, no booking CTA

A click-to-call and WhatsApp button on every page, plus a clear "Book a Consultation" path from the homepage.

02

Blank Google search listing

SEO-written titles & descriptions for every page, targeting the searches patients are already typing.

03

Testimonials buried at the bottom

Patient stories surfaced near every consultation CTA and on the relevant service pages.

04

Squarespace, frozen since 2021

A modern custom-built site — fast, mobile-first, and ready for booking automation as the practice grows.

05

Unnamed images, no alt text

Descriptive, SEO-friendly image content — so the clinic's own photos help it get found, not just decorate the page.

06

No structured data for a medical practice

Physician & LocalBusiness schema so Samatvam is positioned properly for local and Google Maps search.
